Seunghun Baek, Hyeongjun Kim, and Heesoo Yang were selected for the NRF Master's Research Fellowship program.

The fellowship supports full-time master's students with one year of research funding for thesis-centered projects.

Selected students receive 12 million KRW per person for one year.
