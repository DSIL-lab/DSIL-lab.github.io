Minho Park (combined M.S./Ph.D. student, Year 2) received a Best Paper Award at the Korean Conference on Semiconductors (KCS) 2025.

The awarded work addresses reliable 2T0C operation through top-gate oxide semiconductor FET design with reduced capacitive coupling.
