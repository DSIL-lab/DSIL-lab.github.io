Students Guhyun Ho, Hyeonjin Lee, and Nahyeon Kim were selected for the NRF Master's Research Fellowship program.

The fellowship supports full-time master's students with research funding for one year.

Selected students receive 12 million KRW per person for one year.

Program details: [NRF Fellowship Information](https://www.nrf.re.kr/biz/info/info/view?menu_no=378&biz_no=588)
