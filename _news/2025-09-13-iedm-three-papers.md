Three student-led papers from our lab were accepted to the IEEE International Electron Devices Meeting (IEDM) 2025.

The accepted papers are:

- Thermal Evaluation and Comparison of CAA and GAA Indium Tin Oxide Vertical Channel Transistors (Guhyun Ho)
- Monolithic 3D Integration of III-V HEMTs on Glass Using Ultra-Thin Dielectric Bonding Layer: A High-Frequency and Low-Loss Active Glass Platform for Sub-THz Applications (Yurim Choi)
- Active BSCDN Benchmark Framework with Backside-Compatible CNFET Logic Technology (Yehyun Shin, Minho Park)

These results were achieved through global collaboration with Stanford University, KAIST, and UNIST partners.
