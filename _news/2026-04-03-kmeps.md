- Title: Electrohydrodynamic Jet-Printed Coplanar Waveguide Transmission Lines for UCIe-Based High-Speed Heterogeneous Systems

Hyeongjun Kim (combined M.S./Ph.D. student, Year 2) received a Best Presentation Award at KMEPS 2026.

The award recognized work on electrohydrodynamic jet-printed coplanar waveguide transmission lines for UCIe-based high-speed heterogeneous systems.
