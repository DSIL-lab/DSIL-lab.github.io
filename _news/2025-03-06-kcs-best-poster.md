Nahyeon Kim (combined M.S./Ph.D. student, Year 2) received an On-site Best Poster Award at KCS 2025.

The presentation focused on quasi-coaxial through-via architectures for 3D-printed antenna-in-package substrates targeting 5G-Advanced systems.
