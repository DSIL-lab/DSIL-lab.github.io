- Title: Dual-gate carbon nanotube thin-film transistors with printed channel and passivation interlayer on plastic foil ([link](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10630846))

Carbon nanotubes are highly promising for printed transistors due to their high carrier mobility and material stability. This study addresses leakage and controllability limitations by combining dual-gate operation with an inserted polymer interlayer.

The fabricated devices show stable high-performance operation and provide a practical pathway for high-performance printed circuit platforms.
