- Title: Back-end-of-line-compatible passivation of sulfur vacancies in MoS2 transistors using electron-withdrawing benzenethiol ([link](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/full/10.1021/acsnano.4c12927))

The study identifies sulfur-vacancy-driven degradation mechanisms in MoS2 devices and develops a low-temperature recovery route based on pentafluorobenzenethiol chemistry.

The approach is compatible with BEOL integration flows and supports scalable high-performance 2D semiconductor electronics.
