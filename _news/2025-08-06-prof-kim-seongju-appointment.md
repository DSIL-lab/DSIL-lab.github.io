Dr. Seongju Kim was appointed as an Assistant Professor at Hanbat National University (effective Sep 1, 2025).

Before this appointment, Dr. Kim earned his Ph.D. in Materials Science and Engineering at POSTECH and worked as a postdoctoral researcher in Electrical Engineering at UNIST.

His research has focused on thermal management and machine-learning-based optimization in advanced packaging. At Hanbat National University, he plans to continue research and education in AI-enabled optimal design for packaging technologies.
