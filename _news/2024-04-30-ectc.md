- Title: Micro-3D-printed packaging substrates with embedded through holes for organic interposers ([link](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10565076))

Semiconductor system performance is increasingly determined by how chips are interconnected through packaging technology. In this work, we proposed a new process to <strong><u>fabricate packaging substrates with through-holes using micro 3D printing</u></strong>.

Fine through-holes were formed without etching, which reduced process complexity and increased design flexibility. We also developed a reliable inner-hole metallization process and demonstrated broad applicability to advanced packaging substrates.

This work was supported by NRF and IITP.
