Our lab was selected for the Quantum Core Technology Development Program.

The project develops an FPGA-based RF front-end hardware platform capable of wideband transmit/receive operation.

By integrating LO, filter, amplifier, and control modules, the program aims to establish common core technologies for generating, receiving, and processing diverse RF signals for quantum system control.
