Sumin Hong (M.S. student, Year 1) received a Best Presentation Award in the graduate oral session at the KFPE 2024 Fall Conference.

The award-winning work focused on top-gate MoS2 field-effect transistors enabled by optimized gate-dielectric seed layers.
