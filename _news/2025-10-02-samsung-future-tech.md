Our lab was selected for the Samsung Future Technology Program.

This project targets vertically integrated V-die packaging technologies for ultra-high-bandwidth AI accelerators.

The research will combine bonding technologies, thermo-mechanical-electrical design, architecture development, and AI-based optimization to improve I/O scalability, thermal performance, and overall architectural scalability.
