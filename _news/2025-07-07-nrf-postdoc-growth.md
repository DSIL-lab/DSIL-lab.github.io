Dr. Haksoon Jung was selected for the NRF Postdoctoral Growth Support Program.

This program, supported by the Ministry of Education and NRF, helps postdoctoral researchers grow into independent investigators through structured mentoring.

It provides up to 70 million KRW per year for as long as three years.
