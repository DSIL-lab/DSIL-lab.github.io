- Title: Indium Tin Oxide Vertical Channel Transistors for Scaled 4F2 2T0C Gain Cell Memory with Etched Sidewall Cleaning ([link](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/11381590))

This work analyzes how sidewall contamination introduces additional defect states in the channel and degrades RC, SS, and ID characteristics.

By applying wet cleaning after hole etch, the study demonstrates major recovery of RC, SS, and ID, and stable scaling down to around 150 nm hole dimensions.

The process was further validated in 2T0C DRAM operation, demonstrating 2-bit operation and charge retention beyond 160 seconds.
