Dr. Yongwoo Lee was selected for the NRF Domestic Postdoctoral Fellowship program.

The program supports early-career Ph.D. researchers so they can sustain high-level research at the beginning of their careers.

This fellowship provides 60 million KRW per year for two years.

Program details: [NRF Program Information](https://www.nrf.re.kr/biz/info/info/view?menu_no=378&biz_no=352)
